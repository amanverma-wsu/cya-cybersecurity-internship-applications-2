## Platforms Used

- **Jobright AI** — Primary platform for discovering and tracking applications (98 tracked)
- **LinkedIn** — Networking and direct applications
- **Company career pages** — Direct applications for specific roles
- **USAJobs** — Federal government positions (CISA)
- **Handshake** — University-specific postings via WSU

## Key Observations

- The most competitive roles (Amazon, PayPal) opened in late summer and closed quickly, reinforcing the importance of applying early in the cycle.
- Many strong cybersecurity internships require U.S. citizenship or security clearance eligibility, which narrowed some options.
- Compensation varied widely — from $18/hr (Calix) to $102/hr (Amazon) — often correlating with company size and location.
- DevOps and DevSecOps roles showed strong overlap with my CI/CD and cloud infrastructure skills.
- Application security roles aligned most closely with my bug bounty and vulnerability research background.
